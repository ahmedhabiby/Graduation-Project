Luxor Temple & Karnak

Two monumental temple complexes connected by the revived Avenue of Sphinxes.

Overview

Karnak's vast pylons, obelisks, and hypostyle hall testify to centuries of additions. Luxor Temple glows at night with statues, colonnades, and chapels along the Nile embankment.

Things to See & Do

  • Walk the Avenue of Sphinxes between the sites
  • See the Great Hypostyle Hall at Karnak
  • Visit Luxor Temple after sunset for night lighting

Getting There

Fly to Luxor (LXR) or arrive by Nile cruise. Taxis and ride-hailing available; many hotels are walkable to Luxor Temple.

Tips

  • Buy a combo ticket if available; hire a licensed guide
  • Arrive early or late to avoid midday heat

Practical Info

  • Opening hours: typically 6:00–17:00 (seasonal variations)
  • Tickets: Separate tickets for Karnak and Luxor; night ticket extra
  • Dress for sun; water and hat essential; tripod rules vary
