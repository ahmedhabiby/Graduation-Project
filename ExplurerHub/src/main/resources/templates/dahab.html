Highlight — The Red Sea

Dahab

A laid‑back town famed for the Blue Hole, windsports, and cozy shore dives.

Overview

Chill cafes line the waterfront. Nearby reefs and canyons make for accessible shore entries and epic free‑diving spots.

Top Experiences

  • Dive or snorkel the Blue Hole
  • Kitesurf and windsurf in the lagoons
  • Day trips to Blue Lagoon and Abu Galum
