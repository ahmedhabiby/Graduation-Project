Region

The Med

Alexandria's storied corniche, coastal fortresses, and beach towns along Egypt's Mediterranean.

Highlights

Citadel of Qaitbay

Historic fortress overlooking Alexandrias harbor.

Bibliotheca Alexandrina

Modern library with museums and exhibitions.

Montaza Gardens

Palatial gardens, palm avenues, sea views.

North Coast

Sandy beaches stretching toward Marsa Matrouh.

Activities

  • Stroll the corniche and harborside cafes
  • Visit museums: Greco-Roman, National Museum of Alexandria
  • Beach days and coastal drives on the North Coast
  • Seafood dining and cultural festivals

Best Time to Visit

April–June and September–October for mild weather and calmer seas.

Travel Tips

  • Pack a light jacket for evening sea breezes.
  • Many sites are walkable; taxis and ride‑hailing are widely available.
  • Weekends can be busy; arrive early for popular beaches.
