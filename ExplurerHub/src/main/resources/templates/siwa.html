Siwa Oasis

Crystal salt pools, mud-brick architecture, and the ancient Oracle temple.

Overview

Near the Libyan border, Siwa blends Berber heritage with eco-friendly retreats, palm groves, and mirror-like salt lakes.

Top Experiences

  • Float in salt pools and soak in hot springs
  • Explore the Oracle of Amun at Aghurmi
  • Dune drives into the Great Sand Sea
