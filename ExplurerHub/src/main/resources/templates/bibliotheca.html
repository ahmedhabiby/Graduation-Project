Bibliotheca Alexandrina

A striking circular library with museums, planetarium, and rotating exhibitions.

Overview

Opened in 2002, the Bibliotheca revives the spirit of the ancient Library of Alexandria with modern architecture and cultural programming.

Things to Do

  • Tour the main reading hall
  • Visit the Manuscripts and Antiquities museums
  • Attend a talk, exhibition, or film screening
