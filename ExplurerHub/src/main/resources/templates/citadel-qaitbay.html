Citadel of Qaitbay

A seaside fortress guarding Alexandria's harbor, built on the site of the ancient lighthouse.

Overview

Built in 1477 CE, the citadel offers sweeping sea views and a glimpse into Alexandria's maritime defenses.

Things to Do

  • Walk ramparts for harbor panoramas
  • Explore the inner courtyard and exhibits
  • Sunset photos along the corniche
