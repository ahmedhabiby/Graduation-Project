Highlight — The Nile

Valley of the Kings

Intricately painted tombs of pharaohs, including the famed tomb of Tutankhamun.

Overview

Set within stark hills on the West Bank, the Valley shelters dozens of royal tombs spanning New Kingdom dynasties.

Notable Tombs

  • KV62 — Tutankhamun
  • KV11 — Ramesses III
  • KV17 — Seti I (separate ticket)

Tips

  • Standard ticket covers a rotating selection of tombs
  • Photography rules vary; check signage
  • Go early for cooler temperatures
