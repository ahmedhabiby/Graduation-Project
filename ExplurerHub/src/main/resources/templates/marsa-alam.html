Marsa Alam

A quieter stretch of coast with excellent reefs and marine encounters.

Overview

Marsa Alam offers pristine reefs with fewer crowds. Dugongs and turtles frequent seagrass meadows at select bays.

Top Experiences

  • Snorkel at Abu Dabbab and Sataya
  • Boat dives at Elphinstone
  • Stargazing on quiet beaches
