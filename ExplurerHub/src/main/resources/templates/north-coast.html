North Coast

Sun‑drenched beaches, turquoise water, and summer escapes along the Mediterranean.

Overview

Stretching west from Alexandria, the North Coast features resort towns and tranquil coves with clear water ideal for families.

Things to Do

  • Beach days and water sports
  • Road‑trip to Marsa Matrouh
  • Seafood dining and seaside walks
