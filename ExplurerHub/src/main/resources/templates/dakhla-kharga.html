Dakhla & Kharga

Historic oases with traditional hamlets, ancient sites, and serene desert vistas.

Overview

Dakhla's mud-brick architecture and Kharga's desert monasteries offer a quieter, cultural side of the Western Desert.

Top Experiences

  • Walk historic villages and palm gardens
  • Visit early Christian monasteries and tombs
  • Enjoy starry night skies far from city lights
