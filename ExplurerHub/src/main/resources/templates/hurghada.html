Hurghada

A classic Red Sea resort city with marinas, family hotels, and bustling dive scene.

Overview

Hurghada stretches along sandy coastlines with easy boat access to offshore reefs and islands—ideal for beginners and families.

Top Experiences

  • Snorkel tours to Giftun and Orange Bay
  • Intro and certified dives with reputable centers
  • Evening strolls in the marina and El Mamsha
