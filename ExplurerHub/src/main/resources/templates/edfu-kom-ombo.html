Edfu & Kom Ombo

Two riverside temples: Edfu, Egypt's best-preserved, and Kom Ombo, dedicated to Sobek and Horus.

Overview

Edfu’s reliefs and towering pylons offer a cinematic look at Ptolemaic temple architecture, while Kom Ombo’s riverside setting is perfect at sunset.

Things to See

  • Sanctuary and mammisi at Edfu
  • Crocodile Museum at Kom Ombo
  • Nile views from temple terraces
