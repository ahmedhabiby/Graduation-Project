Region

The Red Sea

World-famous coral reefs, calm bays, and resort towns perfect for sun, sea, and water sports.

Highlights

Hurghada

Marinas, dive boats, and family-friendly resorts with house reefs.

Sharm El-Sheikh

Gateway to Ras Mohammed National Park and lively Naama Bay.

Dahab

Chilled vibe, Blue Hole diving, kitesurf and windsurf lagoons.

Marsa Alam

Pristine reefs; chance to spot dugongs and sea turtles.

Activities

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling at reefs and wrecks
  • Boat trips, glass-bottom boats, parasailing
  • Desert safaris, quad biking, Bedouin dinners
  • Kitesurfing and windsurfing hotspots (Dahab, Safaga, El Gouna)

Best Time to Visit

March–June and September–November offer warm water and pleasant air temps.

Travel Tips

  • Choose reputable dive centers; check certification and group sizes.
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen; respect marine life and protected areas.
  • Windier seasons favor kitesurfing; book gear or lessons ahead.
