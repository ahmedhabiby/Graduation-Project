Aswan & Philae

Granite quarries, Nubian heritage, and the island Temple of Isis relocated to Agilkia.

Overview

Aswans laid‑back rhythm and granite boulders contrast with elegant feluccas and island temples. Philae, saved from flooding, is reached by boat.
