Abu Simbel

Colossal rock‑cut temples of Ramses II and Nefertari on Lake Nasser.

Overview

In the 1960s, an international effort moved the temples to higher ground, preserving the aligned inner sanctum that lights up during biannual solar phenomena.

Visiting

  • Accessible by flight from Aswan or long day trip by road
  • Arrive early for softer light and fewer crowds
