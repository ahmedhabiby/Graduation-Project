Region

The Nile

Sail past riverbank temples, palm-lined villages, and timeless landscapes between Luxor and Aswan.

Highlights

Luxor Temple & Karnak

Linked by the Avenue of Sphinxes—vast pylons, obelisks, and sacred lake.

Valley of the Kings

Intricately painted royal tombs including Tutankhamun’s resting place.

Edfu & Kom Ombo

Horus’s best-preserved temple; unique double temple overlooking the Nile.

Aswan & Philae

Granite quarries, Nubian culture, and the relocated island temple of Isis.

Abu Simbel

Ramses II’s monumental temples carved into the cliff on Lake Nasser.

Activities

  • Nile cruise (3–7 nights) or traditional felucca day sail
  • Temple tours with licensed guides
  • Hot-air balloon over West Bank (Luxor)
  • Nubian village visit and Aswan botanical garden

Best Time to Visit

October to April for cooler temperatures; summer is hot but quieter.

Travel Tips

  • Book reputable cruise lines; check cabin categories and inclusions.
  • Start temple visits early to avoid midday heat.
  • Carry water, hat, and sunscreen; photography rules vary by site.
