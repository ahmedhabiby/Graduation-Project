Region

Deserts & Oases

Vast dunes, rock formations, and palm‑shaded springs in Egypts Western Desert.

Highlights

Siwa Oasis

Crystal salt pools, the Oracle temple, eco‑lodges.

White Desert

Otherworldly chalk sculptures near Farafra.

Bahariya & Black Desert

Cinder‑cone hills, palm groves, hot springs.

Dakhla & Kharga

Mud‑brick hamlets, monasteries, and desert archaeology.

Activities

  • 4x4 safaris, overnight camping under the stars
  • Soak in hot springs and salt pools
  • Sandboarding and dune trekking
  • Cultural tours of oasis villages and ancient sites

Best Time to Visit

October–April for comfortable days and cool nights; summers are very hot.

Travel Tips

  • Travel with licensed desert guides; permits may be required.
  • Bring layers for chilly nights; carry ample water and sun protection.
  • Leave no trace—respect fragile desert ecosystems.
